Duna Alternate Plywood

In Stock SKU: DUNA-ALT-01

Engineered for superior strength and durability. Our Alternate Plywood features a high-density core composed of alternate layers of premium timber, making it resistant to warping and termites. Ideal for heavy furniture and interior paneling.

  • 100% Termite & Borer Proof
  • Calibrated for Uniform Thickness
  • High Screw Holding Capacity
  • E1 Emission Compliant
Get a Quote

Duna Alternate Plywood is manufactured using a unique combination of select timber species. The core is composed of alternate layers of hard and soft wood, bonded with high-grade synthetic resin. This cross-grain construction ensures exceptional stability and resistance to warping. Each sheet undergoes a rigorous preservation treatment to protect against termites and borers, making it a lifelong investment for your interiors.

Thickness 6mm, 9mm, 12mm, 16mm, 19mm, 25mm
Grade MR (Moisture Resistant) / BWR (Boiling Water Resistant)
Core Material Alternate Hardwood & Softwood
Face Veneer Gurjan / Recon
Glue Urea Formaldehyde / Phenol Formaldehyde
Density 700 Kg/m³
